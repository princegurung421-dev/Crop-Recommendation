Inside the AI

Understanding the technology behind our crop recommendations.

The Model

Our system uses a sophisticated Machine Learning model (Random Forest / Decision Tree) trained on a massive dataset of agricultural conditions. It analyzes patterns in soil nutrients and weather parameters to predict the most suitable crop with high accuracy.

The Data

We consider 7 key factors:

  • Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium: Essential soil nutrients.
  • Temperature & Humidity: Critical environmental conditions.
  • pH Value: Soil acidity/alkalinity balance.
  • Rainfall: Water availability.
This system is designed to assist farmers in making data-driven decisions to maximize yield and resource efficiency.
